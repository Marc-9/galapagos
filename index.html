Galapagos Islands Website

This website will teach you everything you need to know about the Galapagos islands. We will touch on all 18 of the major islands; Baltra, Bartolome, Darwin, Espanola, Fernandina, Floreana, Genovesa, Isabela, Marchena, North Seymour, Pinzon, Pinta, Rabida, San Cristobal, Santa Cruz, Santiago, Wolf, and Santa Fe Many of these islands have Spanish names, which are named after someone. This is because the Galapagos Islands are located near Ecuador. The islands were discovered in 1535 by Fray Tomas de Berlanga and were named after the Tortoise that were found on the islands.
