Espanola

This is the southernmost of all the galapagos islands and its location is the breeding grounds for all of the 12,000 waved albatrosses. This island is also home to many different types of birds; waved albatrosses, mockingbirds, blue-footed boobies, darwin finches, Galapagos doves, and hawks. There is also many different types of land/seas animals on the island. This island definitely is full of different types of wildlife.
