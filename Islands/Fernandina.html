Fernandina

This is the westernmost island in Galapagos and is said never to have been invaded by a foreign species. This island is known for its many different primitive animals. It boasts the largest colony of land Iguanas in Galapagos, you may even see them hunt for food in the clear ocean water. Though the island is known for it give of flightless cormorant nesting site also known as flightless birds. The reason these birds are flightless is because of evolution. These birds have no predators so there wings are evolving for the ocean not for flight. This is a huge sign that proves the theory of evolution.
