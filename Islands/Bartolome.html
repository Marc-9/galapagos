Bartolome

The Islands of Bartolome which is most commonly known for its "Pinnacle Rock", thought the climb to the top may be be difficult if you succeed you receive a beautiful 360 degree view. You can also find the the Galapagos penguins which are the smallest types of penguins and the only type of penguins that live North of the Equator. You can also see sea turtles and even a white tipped reef shark. Like most of the other Islands this too was formed by Volcanic activity.
