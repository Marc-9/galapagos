Santa Fe

Most of the Galapagos Islands were formed by Volcanic Eruptions though they Island of Santa Fe was created by tetonic plate activity. Santa Fe is great for hiking as it is known for its great coves. Its natural habitants are the Iguanas which can grow up to 5 feet long and enjoy to eat the cactus. Though these are normal normal cacti they can grow up to 33 feet high which is the longest of any in the Galapagos islands. Other common inhabitants of the island are Manta Rays, Sea Turtles, and Sea lions. All of which can be found when you are swimming in the calm beautiful waters of Santa Fe.
