Baltra

The small island of Baltra was used during World War II as an United States Army Base.It was used to patrol the Pacific ocean for enemy submarines. After the landing strip was used as an airport to allow for tourist to travel to the Galapagos islands. But Baltra is not within the Galapagos National Park so animals are not cared for. In 1984 the Galapagos land Iguanas went extinct on the Baltra island, however in 1990 more Iguanas were brought over from a neighboring island.
