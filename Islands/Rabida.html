Rabida

As seen in the picture in the left the island of Rabida is known for its unique red color, this is due to the oxodized iron mixing with the lava. Though Rabida isnt just known for its red color it also known for its abudance of rare birds. The island has 9 varieties of finches, larged billed flycatchers, galapagos hawks, brown pelicans and more. On the red beaches its easy to see the group of sea lions enjoying the sun.
