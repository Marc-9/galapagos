Santa Cruz

Santa Cruz is the second biggest island in the Galapagos however it has the largest population of the inhabitted island, 8,000 people. Santa Cruz is also the only island where six differant vegetation zones can be seen. The island is home to the Charles Darwin station for tortoise research and breeding. On the island is pit craters and lava tunnels as well as free roaming tortoises.
