Santiago

The island Santiago was a Salt mining island, though those days have passed, however remains of these old mining facilities can still be seen on the island. Fur Seals were thought to be extinct in 1905 but due to circumstances they have come back, and it is said there are about 35,000 seals. Santiago is a great island for tourist to travel, takes pictures of the island, and swim with the friendly Sea Lions.
