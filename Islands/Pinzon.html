Pinzon

The island of Pinzon is known for its species of Giant tortoises, which were only located on this island. Though over the years only one tortoises remained whose name was lonesome George. He recently died without be able to reproduce and create more Giant tortoises. The reason this tortoises became extinict is due to a rat infestation that would eat the eggs of the tortoises. The island is relatively small but still contains other animals including sea lions.
