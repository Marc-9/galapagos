Isabela

Isabela is the biggest and one of the most volcanically active islands in the Galapagos. The island has five active volcanoes and it is well known for the giant tortoise that live in the cloudforests of Isabela.West of Isabela, you might very well catch a glimpse of whales breaching completely out of the water, as well as playful dolphins. Stingrays, small sharks, and turtles are found in lagoons on Isabela. Wolf volcanoe is the tallest point on the island.
